Sobera


An app that use your camera and the supported filters.
IMPORTANT: you need a SD card.



Swipe right and left to change filters.
Tap once to see buttons and info.
If you take a picture, wait a moment before a change of interface.
When you see a question appear and two buttons on the bottom of your phone, choose 'Sobel' to save a copy of your picture with edge detection filter.

Walls

Climbing seems to be more and more popular those lasts years, and for good reasons!
One of the few sports, with swimming, that put in motion the whole body. The main difference being you can see quick results when you begin to climb.

I do mostly Block, indoor climbing, over big matresses and without ropes. It gives more freedom when on the wall and you don't have the live of someone else in your hands.
However I am also fond of outdoor climbing. It usually takes place in beautiful area with amzing atmosphere.
Mountains

Leaving almost all sign of human civilization with a simple backpack for a few days and walk your way to the top of one, two or more mountains? Yes, that is something I like a lot.
Alpes and Pyrénées in France, around Dazaifu and in the prefecture of Gifu in Japan, I have seen enough to know I want to see more.
Countries

I had the chance to travel a lot since childhood but I truly realize the beauty of it in 2012 when I went to Canada.
I have been to America (south and north), Europe, Tunisia and Japan. Around 15 countries in my hunting board. Knowing I have seen but a fraction of the world makes me want to run outside and rush everywhere.

At the time I am writing those lines, in the begining of 2021, travel is not the current trend. So I am sharpening my tools to be ready for the time the situation changes back.
Books

Have you ever heard of the parents scolding their child because he spent too much time playing? Well mine scolded me because I was reading too much.
When you start to read a book, you are diving into a new world, with new characters. And the more you are focused, the clearer your imagination can picture what you are reading. However, when you stop reading, you have all the more struggle to get back to reality than you were deep inside the story.
Well, the 14 year old me was very focused.

My favorite authors would be: Pierre BOTTERO, Bernard WERBER, Robin HOBB, D.J. MacHale and Eoin COLFER (non-exhaustive list).


Of course I also read a lot of Comics, Mangas and Light Novel.
